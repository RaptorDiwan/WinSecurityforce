About Win Security Force
"We don't just protect people.
We protect their peace of mind."

Win Security Force is a premier private security company providing close protection, personal escort, and event security solutions across India. Our team consists of highly trained ex-military and law enforcement professionals who operate with precision, discretion, and absolute professionalism.

Founded with a mission to make elite-level protection accessible to individuals, families, and businesses, we bring the same standards used for high-profile principals to every client engagement — regardless of scale.

We operate under strict confidentiality protocols, are fully licensed under the Private Security Agencies Regulation Act (PSARA), and maintain complete operational transparency with our clients at all times.

Frequently Asked

QUESTIONS &
ANSWERS

How quickly can you deploy an officer? ▶
For standard bookings, we confirm within the hour. For emergency situations, we can deploy a verified officer within 2–4 hours of payment confirmation, subject to availability in your city.
Are officers in uniform or plain-clothed? ▶
Both options are available. Uniformed officers are ideal for events and formal situations; plain-clothed officers are preferred for discreet personal protection and family escort. Specify your preference while booking.
Do you provide female security officers? ▶
Yes. We have trained female officers available for ladies' personal security, children's escort (especially for daughters), and family protection assignments. Please mention this requirement while booking.
Is my personal information kept confidential? ▶
Absolutely. All client information, operational details, and engagement specifics are kept strictly confidential under NDA. We never disclose client identities or assignment details to any third party under any circumstances.
Do you cover outstation and other states? ▶
Yes. We cover major cities across India and arrange outstation escort for road, rail, and air travel. For multi-state operations, we coordinate with trusted partner agencies and local law enforcement to ensure seamless coverage.
What if I need security for multiple people? ▶
We can deploy a team of officers for family groups or large events. The number of officers is determined during the pre-service consultation based on headcount, threat level, and assignment type. Custom pricing applies.
What payment methods are accepted? ▶
We accept all major payment methods through our secure Razorpay gateway — including UPI, debit cards, credit cards, net banking, and wallets. All transactions are encrypted and fully secure.
Can I book on a monthly / recurring basis? ▶
Yes. For ongoing personal or family protection, we offer weekly and monthly engagement plans at better rates than daily bookings. Contact our operations team directly to discuss a custom long-term protection plan.
